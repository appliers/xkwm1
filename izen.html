Izen



Izen is a Discord bot developed in v12 of Discord.js (Node.js) and holds a variety of commands

Commands


- !help
Shows commands

- !snake
Start and play a game of Snake (Singleplayer)

- !hangman
Start and play a game of Hangman (Singleplayer)

- !connect4
Start and play a game of Connect 4 (Multiplayer)
